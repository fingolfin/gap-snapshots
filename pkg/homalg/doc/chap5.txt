  
  [1X5 Elements[0X
  
  An  element  of an object M is internally represented by a morphism from the
  "structure  object"  to  the object M. In particular, the data structure for
  object  elements  automatically  profits  from  the intrinsic realization of
  morphisms in the [5Xhomalg[0m project.
