  
  [1X8 Bicomplexes[0X
  
  Each  bicomplex  in  [5Xhomalg[0m  has  an  underlying  complex  of complexes. The
  bicomplex  structure  is  simply  the addition of the known sign trick which
  induces  the obvious equivalence between the category of bicomplexes and the
  category  of  complexes  with  complexes  as  objects and chain morphisms as
  morphisms.  The  majority  of  filtered  complexes  in  algebra and geometry
  (unlike  topology)  arise  as  the total complex of a bicomplex. Hence, most
  spectral sequences in algebra are spectral sequences of bicomplexes. Indeed,
  bicomplexes  in [5Xhomalg[0m are mainly used as an input for the spectral sequence
  machinery.

  [1X8.2 Bicomplexes: Constructors[0X
  
  [1X8.2-1 HomalgBicomplex[0m
  
  [2X> HomalgBicomplex( [0X[3XC[0X[2X ) _____________________________________________[0Xfunction
  [6XReturns:[0X  a [5Xhomalg[0m bicomplex
  
  This  constructor creates a bicomplex (homological bicomplex) given a [5Xhomalg[0m
  complex  of  (co)complexes  [3XC[0m  (-->  [2XHomalgComplex[0m ([14X6.2-1[0m)), resp. creates a
  bicocomplex   (cohomological   bicomplex)   given   a  [5Xhomalg[0m  cocomplex  of
  (co)complexes  [3XC[0m (--> [2XHomalgCocomplex[0m ([14X6.2-2[0m)). Using the usual sign-trick a
  complex of complexes gives rise to a bicomplex and vice versa.
  
  [4X---------------------------  Example  ----------------------------[0X
    [4Xgap> ZZ := HomalgRingOfIntegers( );[0X
    [4XZ[0X
    [4Xgap> M := HomalgMatrix( "[ 2, 3, 4,   5, 6, 7 ]", 2, 3, ZZ );[0X
    [4X<A 2 x 3 matrix over an internal ring>[0X
    [4Xgap> M := LeftPresentation( M );[0X
    [4X<A non-torsion left module presented by 2 relations for 3 generators>[0X
    [4Xgap> d := Resolution( M );[0X
    [4X<A non-zero right acyclic complex containing a single morphism of left modules\[0X
    [4X at degrees [ 0 .. 1 ]>[0X
    [4Xgap> dd := Hom( d );[0X
    [4X<A non-zero acyclic cocomplex containing a single morphism of right modules at\[0X
    [4X degrees [ 0 .. 1 ]>[0X
    [4Xgap> C := Resolution( dd );[0X
    [4X<An acyclic cocomplex containing a single morphism of right complexes at degre\[0X
    [4Xes [ 0 .. 1 ]>[0X
    [4Xgap> CC := Hom( C );[0X
    [4X<A non-zero acyclic complex containing a single morphism of left cocomplexes a\[0X
    [4Xt degrees [ 0 .. 1 ]>[0X
    [4Xgap> BC := HomalgBicomplex( CC );[0X
    [4X<A non-zero bicomplex containing left modules at bidegrees [ 0 .. 1 ]x[0X
    [4X[ -1 .. 0 ]>[0X
    [4Xgap> Display( BC );[0X

    [4X * *[0X
    [4Xgap> UU := UnderlyingComplex( BC );[0X
